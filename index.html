ENKEY

EN KEY is a well-designed new edition to an overdue market. EN KEY secures your home, office, or any room requiring a key to enter with it’s easy access capabilities. With easy convenience EN KEY provides a walk up and go methodology for just walking through the door, opening & starting your car, AND easily giving others access to your place. This is a billion dollar industry that’s ready for a change.

Are You Interested Yet?

WHY EN?

“EN KEY” is a product focused on providing an experience from entering your home, customer service with an “Ensmith” provider, ordering a new ENKEY, and even the experience of opening an “En” package. We want to build sustainable long relationships with our consumers to show that we are always thinking and trying to provide a safer environment for them. Our concept is based off of the design of the key and the convenience of the fob.

It's A Good Idea?

My Hands Are Full?

Many people struggle with opening a door due to full-hands.

ENKEY is designed to be a convenient, easy, and secure way for users to access their homes or cars. Through careful planning we have taken the ease of pressing a button to now just pulling/or turning the door knob. Users often fumble with their keys to find the right one to get inside or have lost their key(s) all together. With ENKEY you can have your car & home key bundled together as one as a ring that can be with you at all times.   People often get locked out of their cars and homes due to losing their keys whether it be a key fob or an old-fashioned key made from a locksmith. Users deal with the pressure of misplacing their keys, phone, and wallet all the time. We can relieve people of one of those issues by creating an EN KEY.
